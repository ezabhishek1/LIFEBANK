Organ Donation: A Legacy of Life

One organ donor can save up to eight lives and enhance dozens more through tissue donation. Kidneys liberate patients from dialysis, livers rescue those with end-stage disease, and hearts beat anew in grateful recipients.

Tissues such as corneas restore sight, while skin grafts accelerate healing for burn victims. Each gift transforms despair into possibility.

Blood Donation: The Everyday Miracle

Every two seconds someone needs blood. From accident victims and surgical patients to mothers in childbirth, the demand is constant—and only humans can supply it.

A single pint can save up to three lives. Regular donors form a quiet army of heroes, ensuring hospitals never face an empty shelf.
